About Us

Our Mission

Complex AI Prompt Builder is dedicated to lowering the barrier to creating high-quality AI prompts. We believe prompt engineering is not a privilege for a select few experts, but a core skill every AI user can master. Through structured forms and real-time previews, we help users quickly generate professional-grade prompts and unlock the full potential of AI.

What We Offer

  • 13 Scenario Tools: Covering PPT, writing, AI image generation, video scripts, research, App, Web, UI, role settings, resumes, bid proposals, business plans, and PRDs.
  • Real-time Preview: Generate Markdown-format prompts as you type, WYSIWYG.
  • One-click Export: Copy to clipboard or download as .md documents.
  • Bilingual Support: Independent SEO pages serving global users in Chinese and English.
  • Completely Free: All tools are open and free, no registration required.

Technical Highlights

This site uses a pure static architecture. All data processing happens in your browser — we do not collect, store, or upload any user input. Privacy and security are our top priorities.

Future Plans

We will continuously expand tool scenarios (e.g. code generation, data analysis, legal contracts), optimize multilingual support, and explore community-driven features where users can share and discover quality prompts.
